Flight of the HMS Luminary: Original Soundtrack

Released: January 23, 2026

Created for the community project “HMS Luminary”, for Jelly Hoshiumi’s 2026 birthday.

I was originally approached to make a “Luminary”-inspired spoof of the Star Trek intro (which eventually became the “Luminous Frontier” track), but after watching a draft of the flight video, I just had to score the rest of it. The team did an amazing job on this all around. Happy birthday, Jelly!

The score was created with MuseScore 4 and rendered with Muse Sounds.
Album art by Edengarde.
“Luminary” was composed by Singto Conley.
