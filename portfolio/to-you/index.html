To You (Original composition)

Released: February 7, 2024

Original composition for piano quintet (piano + string quartet). This is my first original composition as a vtuber. It was inspired by a private piano improv session and developed live on stream.

The score was created with MuseScore 3 and rendered with the MuseScore General SoundFont.
